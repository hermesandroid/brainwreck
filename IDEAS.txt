BrainWreck — Ideas for Tomorrow
=================================

🎮 GAMEPLAY
-----------
1. Speed Round Mode — 30 seconds, infinite digits, see how many you can nail.
   Leaderboard: "Fastest Brain" category.

2. Reverse + Mirror modes already exist, but what about:
   - "Sum Mode" — show numbers, you type the SUM. 4 7 2 → 13
   - "Odd One Out" — show 5 digits, one is different color, you type THAT one

3. Streak Bonuses — 5 correct in a row = 2x points. 10 = 3x. Reset on fail.
   Small fire emoji 🔥 next to score when streaking.

4. Daily Challenge — one fixed sequence per day. Everyone gets the same.
   Separate daily leaderboard. "Who memorized today's sequence fastest?"

5. Practice Mode — no scoring, no timer, just relax and train.
   Good for warming up before ranked play.


🏆 SOCIAL / COMPETITIVE
-----------------------
6. Share Your Score — "I just hit Level 7 in BrainWreck! 🤯" 
   Copy-to-clipboard button with score + link. Twitter/X ready text.

7. Head-to-Head — two players, same sequence, fastest wins.
   Could use a simple room code (4 letters). WebRTC or shared server.

8. Weekly Tournament — top 10 on Sunday get a crown emoji 👑 
   next to their name all week.


🎨 VISUAL / AUDIO
-----------------
9. Sound Effects — subtle clicks when tapping numpad. 
   Victory jingle on level clear. "Wrong" buzzer. Togglable.

10. Particle Explosions — when you clear a level, green particles burst.
    When you fail, red shatter effect. Canvas-based, lightweight.

11. Dark/Light Theme toggle. Current is "Matrix Dark". Add "Paper White"
    for daytime play (black text on white, clean).

12. Avatar System — pick from 20 emoji avatars. Shows on leaderboard.
    🐱🐶🦊🐸👽🤖👻💀🎃


📱 TECH / INFRA
----------------
13. Offline Mode — full game works without internet. 
    Queue scores locally, sync when back online.

14. PWA — "Add to Home Screen" prompt. Full-screen, no browser chrome.
    Already has meta tags, just needs a service worker + manifest.json.

15. Multiple Languages — detect browser language.
    Level names in Swedish, Spanish, Japanese etc.

16. Accessibility — screen reader support (aria labels on numpad).
    High contrast mode. Larger text option.


🧠 MORE LEVEL THEMES
--------------------
17. "Drunk Mode" 🍺 — numbers wobble/shake while displayed.
    Only available after 10pm (local time). For fun.

18. "Upside Down" 🙃 — sequence displayed upside down.
    Toggle for chaos mode.

19. Custom Levels — player sets digit count and timer. 
    Share your level code with friends.


💾 PERSISTENCE
--------------
20. Player Profiles — multiple users on same device.
    "Who's playing?" screen. Separate stats per profile.

21. Stats Dashboard — total games, accuracy %, average level,
    longest streak, best day. Mini analytics.
